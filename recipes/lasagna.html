Lasagna

This simple lasagna recipe is perfect when you want a comforting, cheesy meal without spending hours in the kitchen. Just layer, bake, and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles (pre-cooked)
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups of mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 190Â°C
  2. Spread some sauce in the bottom of a baking dish
  3. Layer noodles -> ricotta -> sauce -> mozzarella
  4. Repeat layers (about 3 times), ending with cheese on top
  5. Sprinkle seasoning if using
  6. Cover with foil and bake 25 minutes
  7. Uncover, bake 10 more minutes until bubbly
  8. Let rest 10 minutes before cutting
