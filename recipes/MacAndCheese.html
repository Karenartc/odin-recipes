Mac and Cheese

This quick mac and cheese is creamy, cheesy, and ready in minutes. Comfort food made easy!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil macaroni 8 minutes, the drain
  2. Melt butter un a pot, add milk and cheese
  3. Stir until cheese melts and sauce is smooth
  4. Add pasta to the cheese sauce and mix well
  5. Serve warm and enjoy
