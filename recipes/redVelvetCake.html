Red Velvet Cake

This easy red velvet cake is soft, moist, and made with simple ingredients. Perfect for a quick dessert or special occasion!

Ingredients

  • 1 box red velvet cake mix
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup cream cheese frosting

  2. Mix cake mix, eggs, oil, and water until smoooth
